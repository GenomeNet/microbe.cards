About microbe.cards

microbe.cards is a comprehensive database of microbial phenotypes and predictions generated by large language models (LLMs), developed by researchers at the Helmholtz Centre for Infection Research.

Motivation

The microbial world is vast and complex, with countless species exhibiting diverse characteristics. While experimental data exists for well-studied organisms, many species remain poorly characterized. We leverage the power of large language models to bridge this knowledge gap, providing researchers with valuable insights into microbial phenotypes.

Data Curation

Maintain a comprehensive, validated database of microbial phenotypes combining traditional knowledge with AI-powered predictions.

Innovation

Advance the field of microbiology through novel applications of artificial intelligence and machine learning.

Accessibility

Provide free, open access to our database and tools for researchers worldwide.
